Risk Management

Professional traders know exactly how much they are going to lose when the trade does not work out. We don't believe in being lucky once or twice. We focus on trading well and trading consistently. Being successfull and making money comes with the combination of many single trades altogether growing your portfolio.

Consistency

To become an professional and profitable trader you need to work consistently using a strategy that works best for you. Because trading comes with a lot of emotions, you are gonna make mistakes. Most people only focus on being right all the time and focusing on making money. What is more important, is knowing how to lose money.. With the tools we provide you will learn to keep an trading journal so that you gain data of your trading and focus on risk management so you can make decisions based on the statistics.
